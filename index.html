Technical Implementation Model

UK Generation Study infrastructure abstracted for replication

UK Generation Study Technical Stack

Sample Collection

  • Source: Umbilical cord blood
  • Alternative: Heel-prick (dried blood spot)
  • Timing: Shortly after birth
  • Consent: Parental opt-in

Sequencing Infrastructure

  • Platform: Illumina NovaSeq 6000
  • Method: Whole Genome Sequencing
  • Accreditation: ISO 15189
  • Network: 7 NHS Genomic Lab Hubs

Variant Interpretation

  • Provider: QIAGEN
  • Variants: 69,844 curated
  • Genes: 500+
  • Conditions: 223

Clinical Workflow

1. Recruitment (Antenatal)

Parents invited during pregnancy, information provided in 10 languages

2. Consent (Postnatal)

Confirmed consent from parents shortly before birth

3. Sample Collection

Cord blood collected by NHS staff, sent to genomic lab hub

4. Sequencing & Analysis

WGS performed, variants filtered against 223-condition panel

5. Results Return

Target: 28 days for positive, months for negative

6. Clinical Follow-up

Specialist NHS referral for positive results (~1% expected)
