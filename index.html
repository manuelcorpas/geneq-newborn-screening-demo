The Three Decisions That Create Governance Battles

Secondary Findings

What do you tell parents about adult-onset conditions? BRCA variants? Carrier status? Every country fights this battle. There's no right answer - only your answer.

Long-term Data Use

Genome data for lifetime? Research access? Recontacting? The UK faced intense public scrutiny on this. Your consent framework will define your programme's future.

"Actionable" vs "Treatable"

Traditional NBS criteria require treatment. Genomic screening often detects conditions with no treatment but "actionable" surveillance. This terminology war delayed UK implementation.
